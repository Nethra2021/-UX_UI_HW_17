Skills

User Research

Throughout the design process, I carry out the user research findings and competitive analysis findings in order to have a solid foundation from start to end of the design project.

User Empathy

In order to make products that please and help people, I understand people on a psychological and emotional level. I do this by observing and engaging with people through user interviews and usability testing and persona creation.

Visual Design

During the UI/UX Bootcamp at the University of Minnesota, I have learned how to appreciate the visual representation of design work, and have learned to draw inspiration from everything around me to produce hi-fi prototypes using Figma.

About Me

Due to that fascination, I studied Computer Science, but at the end of my studies, I felt like something was missing. That’s when I discovered User Experience Design, I learned I could combine my interest in technology and my passion for helping people to create thoughtful products.
